----- Web Pages -----

BrainyHandCreations.com

BrainyHandCreations.com was created for my small crafting business. It's simple HTML and CSS design allows for users to view images of products made by BHC and images of its customers, as well as allow for direct email contact to the business' personal account using Outlook.

JennysBarberShop.com

JennysBarberShop.com was constructed as a site for a barber shop business to display services, contact information, reviews, shop hours and other details about the shop for its customers to have easy access to information that they needed. This site was done completely in HTML and CSS.

ASTERuniverse.com

ASTERuniverse.com was made as a personal project based on my interests and hobbies. The site discusses topics on science, engineering, and technology and allows users to interact with the site by creating user accounts, chat, and make comments on the site directly through Facebook. HTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL were used in its creation.

AgentJennyHodges.com

AgentJennyHodges.com was constructed as a real estate agent site to allow for contact information, direct link to the companies site for property search and information, use of a calendar and other details about the agent and business. This site was quickly and easily made by the use of WordPress for its creation.
